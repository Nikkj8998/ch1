

# CarbonHive — Interactive Product Explorer Homepage

A single-page, futuristic tech-forward website for CarbonHive, an industrial Process Solution Architect specializing in milling, mixing, and air pollution control equipment. The site will feel like an interactive product explorer — not a typical corporate brochure.

## Design Direction
- **Dark futuristic theme**: Deep blue/black gradients (inspired by the uploaded image) with glowing amber (#FFB81C) accents and subtle blue (#005A9C) highlights
- **Interactive & animated**: Scroll-triggered animations, hover-reveal content, glowing card effects, and particle-style decorative elements
- **Typography**: Bold, modern industrial typeface feel using clean sans-serif fonts

## Homepage Sections

### 1. Hero — "Engineering Precision, Powering Throughput"
- Full-viewport dark gradient hero with a large animated hexagonal motif (referencing the "Engineered Lattice" logo concept and molecular structures)
- Glowing amber headline with animated text reveal
- Two CTAs: "Explore Solutions" (scrolls down) and "Book a Lab Trial" (scrolls to contact)
- Subtle floating particle/dot animation in the background

### 2. Interactive Solutions Explorer
- Three interactive solution cards (Size Reduction, Powder Handling, Emission Control) arranged in a hexagonal/grid layout
- On hover/click, each card expands to reveal key details: what it does, industries served, and key specs
- Glowing border effects and smooth transitions — feels like exploring equipment in a futuristic interface
- Icons and minimal animated illustrations for each solution

### 3. Innovation Lab Spotlight
- Highlight CarbonHive's unique "Try Before You Buy" Material Testing Lab
- Interactive process flow showing: Send Material → Lab Analysis → Feasibility Report → Full Proposal
- Each step clickable/hoverable to reveal more details
- Emphasizes de-risking the purchase decision for high-CAPEX buyers

### 4. Industries Served
- Visual grid of industry sectors (Pharma, Food/Spices, Chemicals, Minerals) with icon-driven cards
- Hover effects reveal key use cases and why CarbonHive fits each sector
- Dark cards with glowing amber accent borders

### 5. Why CarbonHive — Trust Signals
- ISO certifications, years of experience, and key stats displayed as animated counters
- Bimodal messaging: technical credibility for engineers + ROI focus for procurement
- Subtle scroll-triggered fade-in animations

### 6. Contact / Request a Quote
- Clean contact form with fields for name, company, industry, and requirements
- "Book a Lab Trial" prominently featured as the primary CTA
- WhatsApp quick-connect button for spare parts inquiries
- Glowing amber submit button with hover animation

### 7. Sticky Navigation & Footer
- Transparent navbar that becomes solid on scroll, with the CarbonHive name in amber
- Floating "Request a Quote" button always visible
- Footer with company info, quick links, and WhatsApp for Spares link

## Interactions & Animations
- Scroll-triggered section reveals with fade and slide animations
- Glowing hover effects on all interactive cards
- Smooth parallax-style background movement
- Animated hexagonal decorative elements throughout
- Counter animations for stats/numbers when they scroll into view
